## About

Welcome to our practical guide on blockchain **token** and **NFT development.** In this guide, we will cover all the essential aspects of blockchain technology, including its underlying principles and mechanisms. We will explore the popular NFT standards, **ERC721 and ERC1155**, discussing their unique features and use cases. 

Additionally, we will walk you through the process of developing smart contracts, from writing and deploying them to testing and integrating them into **decentralized applications (DApps).** This guide will provide you with the knowledge and tools necessary to succeed in the exciting world of blockchain token and NFT development.
